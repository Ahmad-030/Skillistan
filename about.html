Our Journey

From Bold Idea
to Growing Platform

Skillistan was born from frustration and vision. Ahmad saw a recurring problem: talented developers in Pakistan struggled to find quality clients, while businesses abroad couldn't discover them.

So he built Skillistan — a name fusing Skill with -istan, meaning a land or home — to create a home for every person who wants to grow through technology.

2023 — Founded

The idea takes shape

Ahmad launches the initial concept: a platform where Pakistani IT talent connects with global opportunities and local learners upskill affordably.

2024 — Launch

Skillistan goes live

First cohort of learners enroll, the professional marketplace opens, and the first IT service contracts are signed. The community begins to grow.

2025 — Expansion

Scaling the ecosystem

New categories added, AI and automation services launched, and the roster crosses 500 vetted specialists. Client satisfaction holds steady at 98%.

Today — Growing

Building the future of work

10,000+ active members and a vision to become South Asia's go-to platform for IT learning, hiring, and services.
